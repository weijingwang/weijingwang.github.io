Fred the Frog’s Mercuraculous Experience

Fred the Frog was on the road,
He dropped his thermometer.
The thermometer broke,
And some Mercury showed.

Mercury is poisonous,
It can make you sick.
It dulls your senses quite a bit,
Mercury will also make you feel unfit

Fred gaped at his broken item,
And that did not excite him.
He stared for a minute,
He looked at the time.
And he started to feel like he was ninety-nine.

Mercury can be a gas,
So you can actually inhale it.
As said before, it dulls your senses,
There are more consequences.

Fred started to feel weak,
He was not at his peak.

Mercury is useful.
But to be truthful,
It is quite doomful.
